# Success Rate Performance Monitor (Transaction Success Rate)

## Overview

The **Success Rate Performance Monitor** is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to provide end-to-end visibility into transaction processing performance across multiple payment products/channels. It enables operational and business teams to monitor transaction health, identify service disruptions, and proactively address exceptions before they significantly impact customers or business performance.

> **Disclaimer:** All product/channel names used in this dashboard have been anonymized and replaced with fictional names to protect confidential business information. The data model, visualizations, and analytical capabilities remain representative of the original solution.

---

## 1. Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to provide a centralized view of transaction processing performance and support data-driven operational decision-making.

The dashboard helps stakeholders to:

* Monitor transaction success rates across all payment products.
* Detect underperforming products and markets in near real time.
* Identify transaction exceptions and operational anomalies.
* Measure overall platform reliability.
* Support continuous improvement initiatives through performance tracking.
* Enable faster incident investigation using drill-down capabilities.

By consolidating operational metrics into a single dashboard, business teams can quickly understand where failures occur and prioritize corrective actions that improve customer experience.

---

## 2. Key Performance Indicator (KPI)

### Primary KPI

**Transaction Success Rate Target: 99.90%**

The dashboard measures the percentage of successfully completed transactions against the total number of attempted transactions.

**Formula**

```text
Success Rate =
(Successful Transactions ÷ Total Transactions) × 100
```

The dashboard continuously tracks:

* Overall Success Rate
* Total Transactions
* Successful Transactions
* Total Exceptions
* Daily Success Rate Trend
* Monthly Success Rate Performance
* Product-Level Success Rate

Products performing below the **99.90%** target are easily identified, allowing operational teams to investigate root causes and restore service quality.

---

## 3. Insights Generated from the Dashboard

The dashboard enables several operational and business insights, including:

### Transaction Performance Monitoring

* Monitor overall transaction health across all payment products.
* Compare product performance against the organizational target of **99.90%**.
* Identify products consistently meeting or missing service expectations.

### Exception Analysis

* Determine which products generate the highest number of failed transactions.
* Analyze exception distribution by transaction type.
* Prioritize operational improvements based on exception volume.

### Trend Analysis

* Track daily fluctuations in transaction success rates.
* Identify recurring patterns or temporary service degradation.
* Detect periods of instability that may require further investigation.

### Product Performance Comparison

* Compare transaction volumes alongside success rates.
* Understand whether high-volume products experience proportionally higher failures.
* Benchmark product reliability across the platform.

### Operational Decision Support

The dashboard supports operational teams by enabling them to:

* Quickly identify emerging issues.
* Prioritize incident response.
* Monitor the impact of system improvements.
* Report performance against service targets.
* Make informed decisions using real-time operational metrics.
